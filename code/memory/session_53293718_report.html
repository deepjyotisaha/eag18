The XUV 3XO presents a well-rounded package with a strong emphasis on performance, safety, and modern features. It offers both petrol and diesel engine options, coupled with manual and automatic transmission choices, broadening its appeal to a diverse range of buyers. Key highlights include:

  • Crash Test Performance: Achieved a notable score of 29.36 out of 32 for adult occupant protection.
